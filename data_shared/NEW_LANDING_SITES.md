# New Landing Sites Documentation

## Overview

Three new landing sites have been added to the Somalia Fisheries Training Program for field exercises and monitoring operations:

1. **Casco Port**
2. **China Port**
3. **Panama Port**

## Landing Site Details

### Casco Port

- **Location**: Mogadishu region
- **Type**: Active fishing port
- **Characteristics**: Diverse species composition, moderate landing volumes
- **Usage**: Daily monitoring operations and field training exercises
- **Target Species**: Yellowfin tuna (YFT), Kawakawa (KAW), and various reef fish

### China Port

- **Location**: Mogadishu region
- **Type**: Major landing and processing facility
- **Characteristics**: High-volume commercial port with processing infrastructure
- **Usage**: Large-scale data collection and commercial fisheries monitoring
- **Target Species**: Large pelagics including Swordfish (SWO), Yellowfin tuna (YFT), Mahi-mahi (MAH)

### Panama Port

- **Location**: Berbera region
- **Type**: Coastal landing facility
- **Characteristics**: Multi-species landing site with daily operations
- **Usage**: Field training and regional monitoring hub
- **Target Species**: Skipjack tuna (SKJ), Yellowfin tuna (YFT), Albacore (ALB), Bigeye tuna (BET)

## Data Collection

### Module 6 Field Exercises (December 19, 2025)

Participants will visit these sites for hands-on training in:
- Fish measurement and data recording
- Species identification
- Landing site monitoring protocols
- Quality assurance procedures
