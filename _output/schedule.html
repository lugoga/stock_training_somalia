Complete 14-Day Program Timeline

Program Overview

The training program runs from 6-24 December 2025 with 14 days of activities across multiple venues.

Pre-Training (December 6)

Training Needs Assessment (TNA)

Baseline assessment to understand: - Current capacity levels - Specific learning needs - Regional priorities - Previous experience - Language preferences

Outcomes: Customized training modules for each region

Coordination & Opening (December 12-13)

High-Level Coordination Meeting

  • National steering committee
  • Regional authority representatives
  • TAFIRI leadership
  • International partners
  • Budget and logistics finalization

Official Opening Ceremony

  • Welcome remarks
  • Program overview
  • Logistics briefing
  • Participant introductions
  • Opening ceremony celebration

Core Training Modules (December 14-20)

Seven consecutive days of intensive training covering:

  1. Fundamentals - Concepts and theory
  2. Data Types - Understanding fisheries data
  3. Collection - Sampling and protocols
  4. Processing - Database and validation
  5. Analysis - Statistics and visualization
  6. Field Work - Hands-on landing site training
  7. Strategy - National framework development

Practical Training (December 21-23)

Research Vessel Exercises

Three-day intensive offshore training: - Vessel operations - Trawl survey methods - Onboard data collection - Species identification - Fish handling and sampling - Real-time assessment practice

Note: Capacity-limited to 24 participants selected from modules 1-7

Graduation (December 24)

Closing Ceremony

  • Certificate awards
  • Participant testimonials
  • Commitments for implementation
  • Celebration

Important Dates to Remember

  • December 6 - Pre-training assessment deadline
  • December 12 - Confirmation of participants and materials
  • December 13 - Opening ceremony
  • December 20 - Vessel selection announcement
  • December 24 - Final certificates issued

Venue Information

Regional Hubs (Modules 1-7)

  • Berbera, Borama, Mogadishu, Kismayo, Baidoa, Galkacyo
  • Contact: Regional coordinators

Landing Sites (Module 6)

  • Major fishing ports in each region
  • Contact: Regional coordinators

Research Vessels (December 21-23)

  • Detailed logistics to follow
  • Vessel assignments by 20 December
  • Contact: TAFIRI Training Coordinator

Logistics & Support

  • Transportation: Arranged from regional hubs to venues
  • Accommodation: As specified by regional coordinators
  • Meals: Provided at all training venues
  • Materials: Digital and printed resources provided
  • Support: 24/7 technical assistance available
